The Woods of Arreth

Secrets begin to unfold...

The War to free the kingdom of Calsinor and bring Light back to the Western Kingdoms draws nearer!

House Wudsaress has its army, a combination of the Griffin Riders, the Haven Guard, and the armies of Middonai and Ancor. But before the War for Calsinors freedom can begin, tragedy strikes! The Woods Family finds itself divided once again, falling prey to Malved Winhams evil schemes. While Matthew and Rebecca race to rescue two of their children, the other two undertake a secret quest to find the one part of their army still missing, joined by some old friends and a few new ones.

The Final Battle for Calsinor draws closer, but to win, the Woods children will need to find courage in themselves, trust in family, and the aid of The Guardians of Griffinholme!

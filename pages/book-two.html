The Woods of Arreth

Book Two: The Dwarves of Ancor

The epic family adventure that began with The Woods of Arreth continues!

Three months have passed since the Woods children discovered the world of Arreth and learned that one of them may be the prophesied child who can bring about the downfall of King Malved Winham and free the kingdom of Calsinor from his evil rule. But before they can go to war against Winham, they need an army. While Caleb Woods travels with their father to enlist the aid of the Dwarves of Ancor, his sister and brothers travel with their mother deep under the surface on an important mission of their own. Unknown to the Woods family, however, someone close to them is not what they seem to be.

Journey with the Woods family as they continue their adventures, full of mythical creatures, adventure, and betrayal.
